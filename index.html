  • 5a. Do not abuse your rank; if you are a trusted member, you are trusted, not legally immune to any rule broken. This applies to users and administrators as well.

  • 7a. Do not take matters into your own hands unless you have the privilege to do so. Jailing people will be accepted. If you see someone doing something wrong and you have enough evidence to prove it and there is a staff member on, you must jail first, then explain to the staff member.

  • 9a. Try to be friendly to other people. If people don't like what you are doing, don't say "stay mad" or "skill issue," especially for higher members. It paints our community in a bad light for new people.
